Prevention Tips

Stay prepared and protect yourself by following these essential prevention tips for different types of emergencies.

Storm Prevention
  • Secure outdoor objects that could become projectiles.
  • Keep emergency supplies, like water and batteries, at home.
  • Stay informed through weather updates and alerts.
Fire Prevention
  • Install smoke detectors and check batteries regularly.
  • Keep flammable materials away from heat sources.
  • Plan and practice escape routes with your family.
Earthquake Prevention
  • Secure heavy objects to walls to prevent tipping.
  • Identify safe spots in each room, like under sturdy furniture.
  • Prepare an emergency kit with essentials like water, food, and medical supplies.
Flood Prevention
  • Move valuables to higher levels in your home.
  • Keep sandbags ready to prevent water from entering.
  • Avoid driving through flooded areas.
